" collection: portfolio --- Yiwen Sun received the B.E. degree from Department of Electronic Engineering, Tsinghua University, Beijing, China, in 2017. He is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree with Department of Automation, Tsinghua University. His research interests include machine learning, deep learning, sequence learning, spatio-temporal data mining and intelligent transportation systems.
